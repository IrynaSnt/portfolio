Selling ideas,

Professionally, I work at the intersection of sales, community, and human behavior — building pipeline at Forbes, designing gamified features at Adobe, or organizing events that bring 100+ professionals together in cities I moved to two months ago. The challenge
Adobe's hybrid workforce was losing the spontaneous belonging of in-person work. The Adobe Life App was underutilized — employees used it to book desks, not build community. My team was brought in to design features that could sustainably change employee engagement behavior across 6 global office locations.
What I designed
Co-designed two gamified challenge systems grounded in ethical gamification theory and behavioral science — Lunch Break Leap (movement + social connection) and Knowledge Nexus (mentorship + cross-team learning). Applied intrinsic vs. extrinsic motivation research to ensure sustainable engagement rather than shallow reward loops.
Impact
  • Delivered Figma prototype + video demo to Adobe's Civic Partner team
  • Concept selected to directly influence Adobe Life App product roadmap
  • Conducted user research and competitive benchmarking to identify engagement drivers
