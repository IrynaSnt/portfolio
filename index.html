About Me

Selling ideas,
building bridges.

I'm a Business and Cognitive Science student at Minerva University - a program built on the belief that the best way to learn about the world is to live in it. So far I've taken that seriously: six countries, four languages, and enough cultural whiplash to make me very good at reading a room.

Professionally, I work at the intersection of sales, community, and human behavior - building pipeline at Forbes, designing gamified features at Adobe, or organizing events that bring 100+ professionals together in cities I moved to two months ago. I'm drawn to work where the goal is to make someone say yes to something they didn't know they needed.

Off the clock, I train on aerial silks - which teaches you that falling gracefully is a skill worth having.
